Complex tasks are broken into a structured hierarchy of subtasks. The agent creates a plan, allocates steps to sub-processes, then executes them — enabling handling of problems too complex for a single pass.

// Self-Improvement Loop

Reflection & Self-Critique

The agent generates an initial output, then critically evaluates it against quality criteria — identifying flaws, scoring performance, and revising. Produces substantially better results than single-pass generation.
