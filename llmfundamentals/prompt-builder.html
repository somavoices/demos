WHY EACH COMPONENT MATTERS

👤 Role
Sets expertise level. Without it the model answers as a generic assistant — no domain knowledge assumed, no specialised vocabulary, no seniority of judgment.
🔍 Context
Narrows the problem space. Without it the model guesses what you're working on — it may give advice for the wrong stack, wrong scale, or wrong failure mode entirely.
🎯 🚧 Task + Constraints
Tells the model exactly what to do and what NOT to do. Without clear scope the model broadens its answer, adding noise and missing the specific bottleneck you care about.
📋 Output Format
Makes output parseable and consistent. Essential for automation — without it you get prose that varies per run, is hard to diff, and cannot be reliably post-processed.
